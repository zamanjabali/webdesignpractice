the tiger

a wild animal

The tiger is a large and powerful wild cat known for its strength, beauty, and distinctive orange coat with black stripes. It is a carnivorous mammal and the largest species in the cat family. Tigers are found mainly in parts of Asia, including India, which is home to the Bengal tiger.
